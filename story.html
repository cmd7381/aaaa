The Alatalo Story

Alatalo Bros based in Albury-Wodonga with a building history spanning 30 years is synonymous with attention to detail, personalised service and quality workmanship and a reputation as a progressive property developer.

The company is family owned and operated and dedicated to providing quality homes and residential subdivisions throughout Albury-Wodonga, Wagga Wagga & Shepparton.

Our exclusive land bank is spread over 12 individual estates. The ease of dealing with builder and the developer of the land makes the decision process an enjoyable hassle free experience. Decide on a block by browsing our extensive land database in Albury-Wodonga and Wagga Wagga.

If you are building your dream home Alatalo Bros can make your dream a reality.
